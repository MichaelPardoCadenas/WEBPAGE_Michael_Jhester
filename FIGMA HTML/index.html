Retro 8-bit Computers

Retro 8-bit computers, like the Commodore 64, ZX Spectrum, and Apple II, revolutionized home computing in the late 20th century. Known for their iconic graphics, chiptune soundtracks, and groundbreaking games, these machines were the birthplace of modern computing and gaming culture. They remain cherished by enthusiasts for their simplicity, innovation, and nostalgic charm.
